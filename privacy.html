Privacy Policy for 2703PODS

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Coleman Accelerated Transport LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the 2703PODS mobile application (the "App"). The App is a work tool provided to authorized drivers for submitting Proof of Delivery (POD) documentation. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary to perform its function: receiving signed Proof of Delivery documents from authorized drivers and routing them to our office.

We collect:

  • Account credentials: A username and password assigned by us so the App knows you are an authorized driver.
  • Submission data: The Trip number and Container number you enter for each POD submission.
  • Photographs: The images you capture of signed Proof of Delivery documents.
  • Submission timestamp: The date and time each POD is submitted.
  • Basic technical information: Standard device-level information (such as device type and operating system version) needed for the App to function properly.

The App does not collect: your precise location, contacts, calendar, browsing history, advertising identifiers, biometric data, or any information unrelated to POD submission.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Authenticate you as an authorized driver;
  • Route your POD submissions (Trip number, Container number, and photographs) to our office inbox for billing and recordkeeping;
  • Display your past submissions back to you within the App;
  • Maintain operational records of completed deliveries as required for our business.

We do not use the information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating our transportation business.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only with the following categories of recipients:

  • Our office: Submitted PODs (including photographs, Trip number, and Container number) are emailed to our internal billing inbox.
  • Our customers: Completed PODs and related trip documentation may be forwarded to the customer who contracted the delivery, as part of standard billing practice.
  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party services to operate the App:
    • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — for user authentication, data storage, and photograph storage.
    • Resend, Inc. — for delivering POD emails from the App to our inbox.
    • Expo (650 Industries, Inc.) — for App distribution and updates.
    These providers process information on our behalf under their own privacy and security commitments.

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties, and we do not share information for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain POD submissions (including photographs, Trip numbers, and Container numbers) for as long as needed to fulfill billing, recordkeeping, audit, tax, and other legitimate business purposes. This is typically several years, consistent with standard transportation industry recordkeeping practices and applicable law. Account credentials are retained while you are an active driver and deactivated when you no longer use the App.

6. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect the information collected by the App, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for data in transit and Google Firebase's managed security for data at rest. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your information protected.
